NoisySignalIntegration.jl

NoisySignalIntegration.jl – A tool to determine uncertainty in numeric integrals of noisy data.

NoisySignalIntegration implements a method to determine the uncertainty in numeric integrals of noisy data on the basis of a Monte-Carlo process. A predecessor of this package was originally intended to estimate uncertainties of band signals in FTIR spectra (see G. Karir et al., 2019), which is reflected in the example given in the usage guide.
